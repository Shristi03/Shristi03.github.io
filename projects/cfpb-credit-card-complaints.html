CFPB Credit Card Complaints Analytics (2019–2026)

Trend + theme analytics on CFPB credit-card complaint data to surface consumer pain points, operational friction, and compliance risk signals for issuers.

Executive Summary

This project analyzes CFPB credit-card complaints over 2019–2026 to identify where and why customers experience recurring friction (billing disputes, fees, fraud/chargebacks, servicing issues). It combines time-series trends, geography, and issue-level drilldowns with narrative/theme analysis to support early warning monitoring and prioritization.

Why it matters (finance context): complaint spikes often precede regulatory scrutiny, reputational risk, operational breakdowns, and avoidable cost in servicing and disputes. A structured monitoring layer enables faster root-cause triage.

Problem & Stakeholders

Business Problem

  • Complaint volumes change over time, but leaders need to know what’s driving the change.
  • Narrative text contains rich signals, but is hard to operationalize without structure.
  • Teams need a repeatable view across issue type, geography, and time.

Primary Users

  • Consumer operations & servicing leadership
  • Compliance / risk governance teams
  • Product owners (fees, statements, disputes)
  • BI/analytics partners building monitoring

Data & Method

Data Inputs

  • CFPB complaint records (credit card-related)
  • Fields used: date received, issue/sub-issue, company, state, channel, timely response, disposition
  • Optional: complaint narratives for theme mining (if available)

Analytics Approach

  • Trend analysis: monthly/quarterly complaint volume & mix shifts
  • Segmentation: by issue, state, channel, and response metrics
  • Theme extraction: keyword/topic signals from narrative text (if present)
  • Action layer: convert insights into prioritization recommendations
Data quality mindset: standardize dates, validate missingness, deduplicate, normalize issue labels, and track coverage differences across time windows (especially around 2020–2021 changes in behavior and disputes).

Deliverables

Dashboard Views

  • Complaint trend lines (MoM / YoY)
  • Issue mix + top drivers
  • Geographic hotspots (state-level)
  • Channel & operational KPIs (e.g., timely response)

Insight Brief

  • Top recurring pain points & what changed recently
  • Where to investigate first (triage framework)
  • Risk/control lens: patterns that warrant monitoring
  • Recommendations with measurable KPIs

Impact & Value

  • Creates an early warning monitoring layer for complaint spikes and theme shifts.
  • Improves root-cause speed by connecting trend → issue → geography/channel → narrative signals.
  • Supports governance-ready reporting by structuring metrics and drilldowns consistently over time.
