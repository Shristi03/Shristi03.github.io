CFPB Credit Card Complaints Analytics (2019–2026)

Complaint trend analytics + narrative theme mining to support risk prioritization, compliance monitoring, and customer experience improvements.

Problem

  • High complaint volumes can signal operational breakdowns, product friction, or emerging fraud/dispute patterns.
  • Leaders need early warning indicators + drilldowns to pinpoint what’s driving complaints.

Approach

  • Data prep: standardize dates, product subtypes, issue labels; remove duplicates; validate missingness.
  • KPI layer: complaint volumes, resolution time, closure outcomes, channel mix, geography.
  • Text analytics: tokenization + theme extraction (topic modeling / clustering) on complaint narratives.
  • Dashboard: executive view + drilldowns by issue, issuer/product, state, and time.

Outputs

  • Executive dashboard with trend lines, heatmaps, and issue distribution.
  • Theme dictionary showing top complaint drivers and how they change over time.
  • Recommendations: reduce repeat drivers, tighten dispute workflow, and monitor emerging risk themes.
